## ğŸ§ª Laboratory Skills

### Essential Lab Techniques

1. **Experimental Design**
   - Identifying independent and dependent variables
   - Controlling variables
   - Determining what measurements to collect

2. **Data Collection**
   - Using measurement tools correctly
   - Recording data with appropriate precision
   - Minimizing systematic and random errors

3. **Data Analysis**
   - Creating appropriate graphs
   - Linearization techniques
   - Calculating slopes and intercepts
   - Propagating uncertainty

4. **Conclusion & Evaluation**
   - Comparing experimental results to theoretical predictions
   - Identifying sources of error
   - Suggesting improvements

## ğŸ¯ Lab Report Tips

### What to Include

- **Purpose**: Clear statement of what you're investigating
- **Procedure**: Detailed enough for replication, with diagrams
- **Data**: Organized tables with units and uncertainty
- **Analysis**: Graphs, calculations, and linearization
- **Conclusion**: Compare to theory, discuss errors, suggest improvements

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

- âŒ Not including units on measurements
- âŒ Graphs without labeled axes or titles
- âŒ Ignoring uncertainty in measurements
- âŒ Not linearizing when appropriate
- âŒ Failing to identify sources of systematic error

The AP Physics 1 exam includes experimental design questions. Key skills:

1. **Design an experiment** to test a hypothesis
2. **Identify variables** to measure and control

4. **Sketch predicted graphs** based on physics principles
