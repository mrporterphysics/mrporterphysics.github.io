## ğŸ“ Study Tips

### Effective Study Strategies

1. **Master the Fact Sheet** - Know every equation, when to use it, and what each variable represents
2. **Practice with Simulations** - Visual understanding is crucial for conceptual questions
3. **Work Practice Problems** - The more problems you solve, the better you'll recognize patterns
4. **Linearization is Key** - Understanding how to linearize data is essential for experimental design questions
5. **Focus on Conceptual Understanding** - AP Physics 1 heavily emphasizes conceptual reasoning over calculation

### Preparing for Assessments

- Review learning objectives before each unit assessment
- Use the SBG practice problems for targeted practice
- Take advantage of reassessment opportunities
- Ask questions during class and office hours
